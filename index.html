About Me

My Sports Journey

From a young age, I've been fascinated by the world of sports and competition. Like Gon Freecss setting out to become a Hunter, I've dedicated myself to mastering multiple disciplines and pushing my physical limits.

My journey began with running, where I discovered the joy of endurance and speed. This evolved into a passion for tennis, where strategy meets athleticism, and basketball, where teamwork creates something greater than the individual.

When I'm not training or competing, I draw inspiration from anime like Hunter x Hunter, which teaches valuable lessons about perseverance, friendship, and the relentless pursuit of one's goals.
